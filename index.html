Justice without reconciliation? War crimes and trials in Europe since 1945

OFAJ program - The Franco-German Youth Office, exchange programme

The overall aim of the program was to explore and discuss the question of war crimes and war crime trials in four countries (France, Germany, Serbia, Kosovo) especially their role in post-warreconciliation processes. The Program was consisted of three phases, the first part has been held in Dachau (Germany) and The Hague (Netherlands). It will dealt with the topics of crimes committed by the German National Socialists during World War II and their impact on societies; the development of international and national criminal law; the establishment and the role of the International Criminal Tribunal for the former Yugoslavia for the reconciliation in the South Eastern Europe. The second phase of project was held in France in March 2016 and the third phase in Serbia and Kosovo, November 2016.
